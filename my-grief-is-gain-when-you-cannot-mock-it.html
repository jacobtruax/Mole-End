An artist based in NYC, Hanna Umin navigates desire, labor, and denial: she desires, she labors, she is denied. She recieved her degree in painting at Camberwell College of Arts, in London. 2013. Past shows include In Styx Silt at Gern en Regalia, Bar Art at Interstate Projects, My Shed Shaped Heart at Hotel Art Pavilion, Kā skan, Tā atskan at No Moon. This is her first solo show in NYC.
